\subsection{Formula}

\begin{displaymath}
\delta = \sqrt{\frac{2}{\omega\mu\sigma}}
\end{displaymath}

$\omega$: is $2\pi$ times the frequency $f$ of operation in Hertz

$\mu$: permeability of the conductor in $\frac{H}{m}$

$\sigma$: conductance in $\frac{mhos}{m}$
